The Ring Tailed Lemur!

On your way to the treetops, you find a Ring-Tailed Lemur on the ground! It runs to a tree and begins to climb, so you follow it. The Ring-Tailed Lemur spends about 50% of its day on the ground, and the rest in jungle canopy. You follow the monkey to the top of the tree where a whole group of Ring-Tailed Lemurs sit. These monkeys stick together in groups and communicate with different vocal calls. The groups of lemurs eat fruit, leaves, insects, and other plant parts.

Watching the Ring-Tailed Lemurs enjoy their meal makes you realize you are hungry! You climb down the tree and head back to camp to eat some food.
