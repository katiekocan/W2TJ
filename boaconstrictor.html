The Emerald Boa Constrictor!

You climb to the top of a tree and find an Emerald Tree Boa! This snake resides high in the foliage of the jungle. When it needs to eat, it goes to a branch just above the ground and hunts small rodents that walk beneath it. The Emerald Tree Boa lives an average of 15 years. It usually weighs about three pounds and can be over six feet long!

After you meet the Emerald Tree Boa, you climb down from the jungle canopy. There is more to explore! Where do you go next?
