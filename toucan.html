The Channel Billed Toucan!

You climb to the top of a tree and come across a Channel Billed Toucan! This bird resides in the canopy layer of the jungle. Though it is a bird, it does not do a lot of flying; it prefers to bounce from branch to branch. The Channel Billed Toucan has a large bill that helps to regulate its body temperature. Its bill can be over seven inches long! It uses its bill to eat fruit.

The Channel Billed Toucan teaches you how to bounce from branch to branch. You follow it through the treetops until it leads you to its nest! It goes inside the nest to rest. You climb down and decide to head back to your camp to rest as well.
