The Okapi!

You wander through the grassy land of the jungle and come across an Okapi! This mammal looks similar to a zebra, with its backside covered in black and white stripes. However, its closest living relative is actually the giraffe. It has a long tongue that it uses to eat leaves, twigs, and fruit. The Okapi enjoys the vegetation that grows where light leaks through the jungle canopy.

While you watch the Okapi feast on the plants, you step on a twig! It snaps and the Okapi runs away. There is more to explore in the jungle! Where do you go next?
