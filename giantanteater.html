The Giant Anteater!

As you journey through the brush, you bump into a Giant Anteater! The Giant Anteater is the largest of the anteater species. It can be up to eight feet long! It feeds on termites and ants using its two-foot-long tongue. It has no teeth! It walks slowly, shuffling across the jungle ground, but can gallop at over 30 miles per hour when it needs to. It can also swim and climb. The Giant Anteater is passive and will avoid threats if possible.

When you bump into the Giant Anteater, you notice it is eating from an anthill. You realize you are also hungry and head back to your camp to get a snack.
