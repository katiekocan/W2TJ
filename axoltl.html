The Axolotl!

When you reach the river, you come across an Axolotl! The Axolotl is a type of mole salamander. It has webbed feet, a tail, and a body fin and remains aquatic its entire life. The Axolotl develops lungs, but uses feathery gills to breathe underwater. The Axolotl can shift color a few shades lighter or darker to camouflage itself. The Axolotl also has a unique ability to regenerate its limbs, lungs, heart, jaw, spine, and even parts of its brain! It eats worms, mollusks, crustaceans, insect larvae, and small fish.

You watch the Axolotl for a moment before you witness it shift color! You quickly lose it in the water after it camouflages. There is more to explore in the jungle! Where do you go next?
